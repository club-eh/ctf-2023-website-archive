Rules

Breaking any of these rules will lead to disciplinary measures, up to and including disqualification (at the discretion of the organizers).

  • 1. Be respectful.

    Harassment, bullying, threats, or any form of discrimination will NOT be tolerated.

  • 2. Do not attack the competition infrastructure.

    If you are ever unsure whether a target is in-scope, open a ticket in Discord and ask! We'll be more than happy to clarify.

  • 2a. Do not conduct online bruteforcing.

    None of our online challenges are meant to be bruteforced, and you will not benefit from doing so. As with our infrastructure, if you are ever uncertain, please open a ticket and ask us!

  • 3. Do not attack other players or prevent others from obtaining flags.

    This is a Jeopardy-style CTF, not Attack/Defense. Players should not interfere with each other.

  • 4. Do not share flags or otherwise aid other players in solving challenges.

    This is an individual competition: teams are not allowed, and sharing information or flags is considered cheating.

  • 5. If you see something, say something.

    Please report any rule-breaking behaviour or misbehaving challenges/infrastructure via the ticket system.
